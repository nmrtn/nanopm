**Acceptance:** A fresh pm-strategy run produces an adversarial challenge with a specific falsification condition (e.g., "fewer than 3 of 10 users in [segment] do [action] within 14 days"). A fresh pm-prd run includes the commits-delta field in the Success Criteria section.
