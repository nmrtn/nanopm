## Pitch
Right after the first verdict lands, close the loop in the same session: a one-keystroke "Want me to turn that gap into a falsifiable bet? [y]" that runs the strategy gate on the SAME repo and shows the bet refusing to complete until the founder names what would prove it wrong. The aha isn't the diagnosis — it's watching the tool refuse his vibes and demand a falsifiable claim, live, on his own product. That refusal is the thing ChatGPT will never do, and it must be felt once, not described.

## Riskiest assumption
That experiencing the gate as a refusal feels like rigor-as-a-gift rather than friction-as-an-obstacle — a solo founder in a hurry may read "it won't let me finish" as the tool being precious, and bail.

## Cheapest test
Wizard-of-Oz it in 5 live screen-shares: after the verdict, prompt the bet gate by hand and watch faces/words at the refusal moment — code each reaction as "this is the point" vs "ugh, let me out."

## Dissent/tension note
Design: exactly ONE felt refusal in the first session beats demonstrating the whole Plan phase — which would re-bury the payoff under steps.
