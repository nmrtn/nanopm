# ChatPRD Pricing — snapshot 2026-06-10

## Free
- $0/month
- 3 chats (limited length), basic AI model, document generation, basic templates

## Pro
- $15/month (or $179/year, 25% annual savings)
- Unlimited chats, premium AI models (GPT-4o, Claude, o1)
- Unlimited documents, custom templates
- Projects with saved knowledge
- File & image uploads
- Google Drive integration, Notion export, Slack integration
- Priority support

## Teams
- $29/month per seat (or $349/year per seat)
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- Centralized billing, team workspace
- Shared projects & knowledge, shared templates
- Real-time doc collaboration, comments on documents
- Linear integration
- Admin controls

## Enterprise
- Custom pricing
- Advanced features, granular data controls, SSO, dedicated support
