## Summary

Solo-builder Sam is a solo founder who is simultaneously PM, engineer, and founder — shipping a product with an AI coding agent in their terminal. The product's architecture, CLI-first UX, and context-compounding memory are all downstream of this persona being the primary user. As of 2026-06-26, the only confirmed user who has run the full pipeline is the maintainer (Nicolas), making this a strong n=1 signal with an open market-breadth question.

## What we know

**Core job to be done**
Functional: run the full product loop (discover → decide → plan → build → learn) without leaving the terminal. Emotional: feel like a real PM process is running alongside them, not like they're winging it alone.
- "Solo founders who code in a terminal (using an AI coding agent) want an autonomous, compounding PM loop — not a Notion page, not a ChatGPT session, not their own head." — docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26

**The trigger moment**
They ship the wrong feature (or a competitor ships what they should have shipped) and the cost of ad-hoc product decisions becomes real. "I already have Claude Code in my terminal — what if it could do the PM work too?"
- "They're in their terminal mid-sprint, staring at a half-finished feature, realizing they've been building on vibes — no clear sense of whether this is the right thing to build next, for whom, or what 'done' actually looks like." — docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26

**Today's workarounds**
A Notion doc that goes stale after week two. A ChatGPT session with no memory of last month's decisions. A Linear backlog created reactively from whatever the last user DM said. Or nothing at all.
- "A Notion doc that goes stale after week two. A ChatGPT session with no memory of last month's decisions. A Linear backlog created reactively from whatever the last user DM said. Or nothing at all — just their own head and git log." — docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26

**How to recognize them (behavioral signals)**
- "They commit ≥3 skill outputs to their repo. They run /pm-run a second time unprompted. They invoke a skill that reads from a prior skill output within 14 days — the habit loop has started." — docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26

**n=1 risk — dogfooding gap**
- "The only person who has run the full pipeline unprompted is Nicolas — the maintainer. The target user is the org. This is excellent dogfooding and a genuine n=1 risk simultaneously." — docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26
- "The gap between 'one person validates this in their own project' and '10 strangers adopt the habit loop' (Objective 1, KR1–KR3) is the gap that needs closing before the personas harden." — docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26

**Confidence**
Evidenced from strategy.md and org.md — but with an n=1 asterisk. Strategy explicitly names "solo founders and small teams adopting agentic dev environments" as the target. Maintainer confirmed "yes — solo builder is the primary."
- "The strategy explicitly names 'solo founders and small teams adopting agentic dev environments' as the target." — docs/strategy.md via docs/personas.md, 2026-06-26
