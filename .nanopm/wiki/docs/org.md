## Ways of Working

The real cadence, not the aspiration:

- **Cadence:** frequent informal sync — DMs / calls when something comes up. No standing weekly, no daily standup, no retro cadence.
- **Methodology:** none formal. The skill pack ships in a Shape-Up-ish rhythm (a feature lands when one of them ships a PR), but there's no cycle/sprint structure and no Shape Up pitch process.
- **How decisions get made:** two friends agree in chat. PRs land when both are roughly OK with the direction (or when one ships and the other tacitly accepts via no review block).
- **Tools of record:** GitHub (`nmrtn/nanopm`) is the single source of truth for code, issues, and PRs. No Linear, no Notion workspace, no Jira. `.nanopm/` artifacts in-repo are the record of PM thinking. DMs / calls are the record of decisions — meaning decisions are not recorded.

## Gaps & Open Seats

Real gaps, named plainly:

- **No external users on record.** Zero recruited cohort members. [AUDIT.md](AUDIT.md) (2026-06-11) demanded `recruitment.md` by 2026-06-14 — at the time of this org map, that file does not exist in the repo. This is the single largest gap and it is not a hiring gap, it's an action gap.
- **No designated tiebreaker.** Both partners decide everything together. On a 2-person side project this works while you agree. If you disagree on a meaningful call (e.g. should the viewer keep shipping or should the freeze hold), there's no documented mechanism for resolving it — the default will be drift, not decision.
- **No decision log.** Decisions happen in DMs/calls and are not written down. The `.nanopm/` artifacts capture PM thinking but not the "we agreed X" moments. A new collaborator (or future-you in 6 months) cannot reconstruct *why* a strategic call was made.
- **No GTM / distribution owner.** Neither of you owns acquisition. [BUSINESS-MODEL.md](BUSINESS-MODEL.md) flags "no GTM, pre-PMF" — that's accurate for stage, but it means when you decide to recruit / market / launch, nobody is accountable for it by default.
- **No "user research" owner.** Same shape — interviews, feedback synthesis, cohort care are an unowned function. The skill pack assumes there's a user-facing PM; in the actual org, that seat is empty.
