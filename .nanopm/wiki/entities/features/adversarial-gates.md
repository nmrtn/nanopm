## Summary
Adversarial subagent gates fire on strategy, roadmap, and PRD skills and refuse to let the skill complete unless a falsifiable bet lands. This forces the user to name what would prove a bet wrong before planning proceeds — one of the two structural claims of the product.

**How it works**
A subagent gate blocks completion until a well-formed falsifiable record exists.
