## Sequencing rationale (one paragraph)

This regen records reality (Discovery DB v0 + the memory-wiki engine shipped) and the founder's call to spend this cycle deepening the wiki — validate the personas ingest pilot, fan entity extraction out to people and features, and harden the substrate (coherence lint, regen debounce, bounded index, dead-code removal) — before recruiting. The honest read: that is the *third* deferral of cohort recruitment, and OBJ1's entire proof quarter is downstream of it. The bet implicit in this sequencing is that a richer, multi-source memory substrate makes the eventual recruitment pitch materially stronger (more "show me my opportunity DB + my people + my features, auto-synthesized"), and that a one-cycle depth investment is worth a tighter recruitment window. The risk it introduces — the one CHALLENGES.md has named every cycle — is that wiki depth becomes the next reason not to recruit, and 2026-09-15 arrives with NEXT untouched. Mitigation: NEXT recruitment carries explicit dates and the capacity check above states plainly that this cycle leaves no buffer for it, so starting recruitment requires cutting NOW scope, not finding free time. If the personas pilot (NOW item 1) reads no-go, fan-out (item 2) does not proceed and that ~1.5 eng-week opens for recruitment immediately.
