## Summary
BMad Method is a full-SDLC, multi-phase, multi-agent framework (v6) — the closest conceptual analogue to nanopm's skill-pack shape, with named PM/Architect persona agents and an explicit "Adversarial Review" phase. Its gating criteria are unspecified and its license/pricing are undisclosed, whereas nanopm's gate is concrete and refusal-based.
